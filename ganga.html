The GANGA Global Autonomous Nautical Garbage Assistant is an innovative project aimed at cleaning marine and river environments using a robotic solution. This autonomous system is designed to navigate water bodies, collect floating debris, and ensure a cleaner and safer aquatic ecosystem.

We Made This Boat Through The Following Materials :

Legos Freelancing, Proclainer Arm, Angle Grinder, Brushless Geared Motor, Expanding Foam, Waterproof Motors/Sensors, Fibre Sheet, Pi Camera, Raspberry Pi, Adeshives, Wires, Nuts, Bolts, Tester, Spanner, Scorer, Rubbing Alcohol, PVE Pipes, Monster Truck Wheel, Remote Control Module, Drone Motor/Propeller, Sonar Module, Particle Wood, Drain Pipes, Batteries, USB Panel, Clamp

The Functioning of the Boat is Really Complicated but We can Simply Say :

This Boat Boat Runs By Its Wheels Attached to The Boat ( 6 wheels in Total ) and Ssing the Procliner Arms the Boat can Pick up the Trash
