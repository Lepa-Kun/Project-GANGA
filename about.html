Our Vision

We envision a future where our oceans and waterways are free from the scourge of pollution. Project GANGA is dedicated to developing autonomous systems that can effectively identify, collect, and dispose of marine debris, making our waters cleaner and safer for all forms of life.

Our Approach

Project GANGA utilizes a combination of autonomous robotics, advanced sensors, and data analytics to tackle marine pollution. Our robots are designed to autonomously patrol waterways, identify and collect debris, and ensure its proper disposal. By harnessing the power of AI and machine learning(AI,ML), we aim to make our systems smarter and more effective over time.
